# AI disclosure

These manuscripts were developed with extensive AI assistance.

## Models and roles

- **OpenAI GPT-5.6 Sol:** proof search, drafting, checking of reductions and
  case splits, and revisions.
- **OpenAI GPT-5 (Codex):** later source review, compilation, file checks, and
  preparation of the public artifacts.
- **xAI Grok 4.5:** critique of an earlier version of the $r=5$ manuscript.

AI output is not a correctness certificate, and review by another model is
not external mathematical review. Both manuscripts should be read as
unreviewed proof claims.

The mathematical statements and arguments are presented in full in the PDFs.
Development-time programs are not proof premises. Readers are invited to
report any gap with the exact page, lemma, and line of reasoning involved.
